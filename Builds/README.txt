Game Description:
Shodo is a top down beat ‘em up. You play as a ninja who infiltrates a Palace. Fight waves of enemies, learning new combos as you go.

Controls:
Controller A, B, start, right thumbstick
Keyboard Z, X, Esc, arrow-keys
